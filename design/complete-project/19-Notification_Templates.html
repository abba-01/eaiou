Available Placeholders

Use these placeholders in your notification templates to include dynamic content:

  • {article_title} Title of the submitted article
  • {author_name} Name of the article's author
  • {reviewer_name} Name of the assigned reviewer
  • {editor_name} Name of the handling editor
  • {submission_date} Date when the article was submitted
  • {review_due_date} Deadline for submitting reviews
