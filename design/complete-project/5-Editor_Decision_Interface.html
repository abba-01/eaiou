Strengths:

The paper presents a novel approach to observer modal dynamics with strong theoretical foundations. The methodology is sound and the results are convincing. The literature review is comprehensive and the discussion connects well to existing theories.

Weaknesses:

Some minor issues with the presentation of results in Figure 3. The limitations section could be expanded to address potential alternative interpretations.

Confidential Note to Editor:

This is a strong paper that makes a significant contribution to the field. I highly recommend publication with only minor revisions to the results presentation.

R2

Reviewer #2

Major Revision
Strengths:

The topic is interesting and timely. The literature review shows good knowledge of the field.

Weaknesses:

The methodology has significant flaws that undermine the validity of the results. The sample size is too small for the claims being made, and the statistical analysis is inadequate. Several speculative claims are presented as established facts without proper epistemic status flags.

Confidential Note to Editor:

This paper requires substantial revision before it can be considered for publication. The authors need to address the methodological issues and properly flag speculative claims.

R3

Reviewer #3

Minor Revision
Strengths:

The paper addresses an important gap in the literature. The theoretical framework is well-developed and the discussion of implications is insightful.

Weaknesses:

Some of the citations are outdated and should be replaced with more recent work. The conclusion section is too brief and doesn't fully capture the significance of the findings.

Confidential Note to Editor:

This is a good paper that needs some minor revisions to improve its impact. The authors should update their citations and expand the conclusion.
