Notification Messages

Customize notification messages sent to users at different stages of the peer-review workflow. Use placeholders to dynamically insert content.

Submission Received

Enabled

Dear {author_name},

Thank you for submitting your article "{article_title}" to eaiou. Your submission has been received and assigned the reference number {submission_id}.

We will now begin the review process. You can check the status of your submission at any time by logging into your account.

If you have any questions, please contact us at {support_email}.

Best regards,
The Editorial Team

Available Placeholders

{author_name} {article_title} {submission_id} {submission_date} {support_email} {login_url}

Reviewers Assigned

Enabled

Dear {author_name},

We are pleased to inform you that reviewers have been assigned to evaluate your article "{article_title}" (ID: {submission_id}).

The review process is expected to take approximately {review_duration} days. You will be notified once all reviews have been submitted and a decision has been made.

You can check the status of your submission at any time by logging into your account.

Best regards,
The Editorial Team

{author_name} {article_title} {submission_id} {reviewer_count} {review_duration} {login_url}

Notification Help

Using Placeholders

Placeholders are automatically replaced with actual values when notifications are sent. Use curly braces around placeholder names.

Message Formatting

Basic HTML formatting is supported in notification messages. Use the formatting toolbar to add emphasis to important information.

Conditional Content

You can create separate templates for different decisions (approve, revise, reject) using the "Customize by Decision" option.

Testing Notifications

You can send test notifications to verify your templates before saving changes.
