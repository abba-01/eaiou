Editor Decision

Major Revision
Editor Comments July 10, 2023

Thank you for your submission to eaiou Journal. Based on the reviews, I am requesting major revisions to your manuscript. The reviewers have identified several areas that need significant improvement:

  • The methodology section lacks sufficient detail for replication
  • The literature review needs expansion to include recent studies
  • The speculative claims in the discussion need better grounding
  • Statistical analysis requires more rigor and clarity

Please address all reviewer comments in your revision. You have 30 days to submit your revised manuscript.

Reviewer Comments

R1
Reviewer #1 Recommendation: Approve
Originality: 4/5 Methodology: 3/5 Literature: 4/5 Impact: 5/5

Scholarly Structure

The manuscript follows the academic structure well. The abstract clearly presents the research question and main findings. Each section flows logically into the next.

Methodology

While innovative, the methodology section needs more detail. The author should explain the participant selection process and provide more information about the analysis techniques used.

Results & Discussion

The results are presented clearly with appropriate statistical analysis. The discussion offers thoughtful interpretation of the findings, though some claims are overly speculative.

R2
Reviewer #2 Recommendation: Major Revision
Originality: 3/5 Methodology: 2/5 Literature: 2/5 Impact: 3/5

Literature Review

The literature review is incomplete. Several key studies from the past two years are missing, particularly the work by Chen et al. (2022) and Patel (2023) which directly address similar phenomena.

Methodology

The methodology has significant flaws. Sample size justification is missing, and the statistical approach is inadequate for the research questions posed. The author should consider alternative analytical approaches.

Discussion

The discussion contains multiple speculative claims without sufficient evidence. The limitations section is underdeveloped and fails to acknowledge key constraints of the study design.

Revision Requirements

1

Methodology Section

Expand methodology with detailed procedures, sample size justification, and clear description of analytical techniques.

2

Literature Review

Update with recent studies (2021-2023), particularly Chen et al. (2022) and Patel (2023).

3

Statistical Analysis

Strengthen statistical approach with appropriate tests and provide effect sizes for all reported results.

4

Discussion Section

Revise speculative claims and clearly mark them as such. Expand limitations section to acknowledge methodological constraints.

5

Response Letter

Include a point-by-point response to all reviewer comments explaining how each issue was addressed.
