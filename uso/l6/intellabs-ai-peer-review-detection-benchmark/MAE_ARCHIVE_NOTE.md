# IntelLabs AI Peer Review Detection Benchmark — L6 archive

**Source:** https://huggingface.co/datasets/IntelLabs/AI-Peer-Review-Detection-Benchmark
**Paper:** arxiv:2502.19614
**Created:** Intel Labs, May 2025
**Acquired into eaiou L6:** 2026-05-04 by Mae per Eric's request

## What this is

788,984 paired human-vs-AI peer reviews for ICLR + NeurIPS submissions across
2016–2024. AI reviews generated by 5 LLMs: GPT-4o, Claude Sonnet 3.5,
Gemini 1.5 Pro, Llama 3.1 70B, Qwen 2.5 72B. Three splits: calibration
(75,824 / 273 MB), test (287,052 / 903 MB), extended (426,108 / 1.4 GB).
Format: CSV.

## Why it's in eaiou L6 (per `feedback_l6_trash_bin.md`)

L6 = provenance archive for deprioritized but valuable artifacts; not active
workspace. This dataset sits here as a reference corpus for SAID-side
detection research and analytical comparison. It is NOT being actively
processed by eaiou's runtime. If/when it becomes active workload, it
graduates out of L6 to a dedicated working location.
