Reviewer #1 Anonymous Aug 2

I've reviewed your manuscript and have several suggestions for improvement:

  1. The methodology section needs more detail, particularly regarding the quantum circuit implementation. Please provide specific information about gate sequences and qubit connectivity.
  2. The discussion of results could be strengthened by comparing your quantum approach with classical algorithms more explicitly. What is the quantum advantage in this specific application?
  3. The conclusion seems rushed and does not fully capture the implications of your work. Consider expanding this section to highlight potential future directions.

Overall, this is a promising study that could make a significant contribution with these revisions.
