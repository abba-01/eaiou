Submission Guidelines

Please ensure your submission adheres to the following guidelines:

  • All manuscripts must be original and not under consideration by any other publication.
  • Follow the journal's formatting requirements for text, figures, and references.
  • Ensure all authors have approved the manuscript before submission.
  • Disclose any conflicts of interest or funding sources.
  • Obtain appropriate permissions for any copyrighted material.
  • Include all necessary ethical approvals for research involving humans or animals.

For detailed submission requirements, please refer to the Author Guidelines.
