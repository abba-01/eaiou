Novel Approaches to Quantum Computing: A Systematic Review

Submitted on: July 28, 2023 • MS-2023-0142 • Research Article

Abstract

This systematic review examines recent advances in quantum computing approaches, with particular focus on quantum error correction, topological quantum computation, and photonic quantum systems. We analyze over 200 papers published between 2018-2023 to identify emerging trends and research gaps. Our findings suggest that hybrid quantum-classical algorithms show the most immediate promise for practical applications, while fully fault-tolerant quantum computing remains a significant challenge despite theoretical progress...
