Reviewer Feedback

Reviewer #1

Approve Overall Score: 4.2/5
Summary Comments

This article presents a novel approach to observer modal dynamics, with strong theoretical foundations and excellent scholarly structure. The originality of the work is commendable, and the discussion section effectively interprets the findings within the broader field.

I recommend acceptance with minor revisions to address some citation issues and clarify the methodology section, particularly regarding the statistical analysis of observer effects.

Strengths
  • Innovative theoretical framework
  • Well-structured literature review
  • Clear articulation of complex concepts
  • Strong discussion of implications
Areas for Improvement
  • Several citation errors in the references section
  • Methodology needs clearer explanation of statistical approach
  • Some speculative claims not properly flagged

Reviewer #2

Major Revision Overall Score: 2.3/5
Summary Comments

While the article presents an interesting theoretical framework, there are significant methodological issues that undermine the validity of the conclusions. The results section lacks sufficient evidence to support several key claims, and the statistical analysis appears flawed in multiple areas.

I recommend major revisions to address the methodological weaknesses, improve the evidence base for claims made, and properly distinguish between empirical findings and speculative interpretations.

Strengths
  • Novel theoretical approach
  • Well-written abstract and introduction
  • Important research question
Areas for Improvement
  • Serious methodological flaws in experimental design
  • Insufficient evidence for key claims
  • Poor distinction between empirical results and speculation
  • Inadequate literature review in key areas
  • Multiple citation errors and omissions
