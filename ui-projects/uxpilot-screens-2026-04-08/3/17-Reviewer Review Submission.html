Article Information

Observer Modal Dynamics in Anomalous Contact

Abstract: This study examines the observer effect in anomalous contact scenarios, proposing a novel framework for understanding modal dynamics when consciousness interfaces with non-standard phenomena. Using a mixed-methods approach combining quantitative measurements and qualitative observer reports, we demonstrate statistically significant correlations between observer expectation and phenomenological outcomes. Our results suggest a bidirectional causality model that challenges conventional understanding of observer independence.
