Editor Decision

Dr. Thomas Williams

Editor-in-Chief • August 12, 2023

Based on the conflicting reviews, I am requesting major revisions to address the methodological concerns raised by Reviewer #2. While the paper shows promise and originality as noted by Reviewer #1, the literature review needs strengthening and the speculative claims require additional substantiation or clearer labeling.

Required Revisions:

Strengthen the literature review with additional references from the last 5 years

Improve the methodology section with clearer measurement procedures

Revise the statistical analysis using the suggested methods

Clearly demarcate speculative claims from evidence-based conclusions

Address the specific concerns about observer-bound variables in section 4.2

Revision Submission

Author Response to Reviews

We thank the reviewers and editor for their thoughtful feedback. We have addressed all the requested revisions as detailed below:

Response to Literature Review Concerns:

We have added 12 new references from the past 5 years, particularly strengthening sections 2.1 and 2.3 with recent work by Zhang et al. (2022) and Patel (2021).

Response to Methodology Concerns:

We have completely rewritten section 3 to provide detailed measurement procedures, including calibration methods and error analysis that were previously omitted.

Response to Statistical Analysis Concerns:

We have implemented the suggested Bayesian approach and included additional robustness checks as recommended by Reviewer #2.

Response to Speculative Claims Concerns:

We have clearly labeled all speculative statements with a standardized notation and added a dedicated "Limitations and Speculations" section to separate evidence-based conclusions from theoretical extensions.
