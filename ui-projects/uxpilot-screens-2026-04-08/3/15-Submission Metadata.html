Abstract

This study investigates the influence of observer presence on anomalous contact scenarios, with particular emphasis on the modal dynamics that emerge when conscious observation interacts with quantum-level phenomena. Using a novel experimental setup that isolates observer variables, we demonstrate statistically significant deviations in expected outcomes when trained observers are present versus automated recording systems. Our findings suggest a previously undocumented interaction between consciousness and certain physical systems that cannot be explained by conventional measurement perturbation alone.
