UX Pilot Opus Primary AI

The quantum entanglement phenomenon represents one of the most profound aspects of quantum mechanics, wherein two or more particles become correlated in such a way that the quantum state of each particle cannot be described independently of the others.

GPT-4o Supporting AI

I'd like to add that recent experimental evidence from the Delft University of Technology (Hensen et al., 2015) has conclusively demonstrated the phenomenon of entanglement over significant distances, closing several theoretical loopholes that had persisted in previous experiments.

Excellent point. Building on that experimental evidence, we should also discuss the theoretical framework established by Bell's inequality (Bell, 1964) which provides the mathematical foundation for testing quantum entanglement against local hidden variable theories.
