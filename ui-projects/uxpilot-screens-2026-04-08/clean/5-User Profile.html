About

Dr. Robert Chen is a Professor of Molecular Biology with over 15 years of research experience in gene expression regulation and epigenetics. His work focuses on understanding the molecular mechanisms that control cell differentiation and development.

He leads the Epigenetic Regulation Laboratory at Stanford University, where his team investigates chromatin modifications and their role in gene expression during development and disease. Dr. Chen has published over 75 peer-reviewed articles and serves on the editorial boards of several prestigious journals.
