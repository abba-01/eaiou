Editorial Comments

Your research addresses an important topic with significant potential impact. However, there are several critical areas that need substantial improvement:

  1. The methodology section requires more detail and justification, particularly regarding your sampling approach and data collection methods.
  2. The statistical analysis needs to be completely revised using more appropriate techniques for your data structure.
  3. Your literature review should be updated to include recent studies published in the last 2-3 years.
  4. The discussion section should better acknowledge limitations and avoid overstating conclusions.
  5. Consider adding a policy implications section as suggested by Reviewer #2.

Please submit your revised manuscript within 60 days, along with a detailed response letter addressing each point raised by the reviewers. I believe with these revisions, your paper has the potential to make a valuable contribution to the field.
