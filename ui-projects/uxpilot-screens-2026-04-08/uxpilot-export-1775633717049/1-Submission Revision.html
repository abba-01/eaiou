Reviewer Feedback

Decision: Revisions Required

Reviewer #1

May 20, 2025

Major Revisions

The paper presents an interesting approach to observer-preserving AI integration, but several methodological issues need to be addressed:

  1. The framework for attribution between human and AI contributors lacks sufficient detail.
  2. The experimental validation is limited and needs expansion.
  3. The discussion of ethical implications should be strengthened.

Reviewer #2

May 18, 2025

Minor Revisions

The paper makes a valuable contribution to the field, but requires some clarifications:

  1. The transparency mechanisms should be more clearly defined.
  2. Additional examples would strengthen the practical applications section.
  3. Some technical terms need better definition for interdisciplinary readers.

Reviewer #3

May 22, 2025

Major Revisions

While the concept is promising, significant revisions are needed:

  1. The theoretical foundation needs to be strengthened with more citations.
  2. The limitations section should be expanded substantially.
  3. The reproducibility claims require more evidence and validation.

Editor's Comments

May 25, 2025

Please address all reviewer comments, with particular attention to the methodological concerns raised by Reviewers #1 and #3. The attribution framework needs significant clarification, and the experimental validation should be expanded. We look forward to receiving your revised manuscript.
