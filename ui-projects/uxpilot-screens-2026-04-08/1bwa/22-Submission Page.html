AI Disclosure Guidelines

Transparency about AI involvement is crucial for research integrity. Please disclose all AI tools used in your research process, including:

  • Text generation or editing assistance
  • Data analysis and processing
  • Code generation or debugging
  • Image or figure creation
  • Literature review assistance

Note: Using AI tools does not diminish the value of your research, but failure to disclose their use violates our transparency policy.
