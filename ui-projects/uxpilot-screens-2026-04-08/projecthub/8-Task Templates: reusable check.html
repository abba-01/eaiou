Intake Interview Protocol

Standardized checklist for initial client interviews

Checklist Items

1. Verify client identity

Check government-issued ID and record details

2. Explain confidentiality policy

Review legal protections and limitations

3. Collect basic case information

Date, time, location of incident, involved parties

4. Document timeline of events

Record chronological sequence with timestamps

5. Identify potential witnesses

Names, contact information, relationship to case

6. Record existing evidence

List documents, photos, recordings already in possession

7. Outline next steps

Explain investigation process and timeline expectations
