Help & Tips

Calendar Sync Tips

  • Changes made in Google or Outlook will automatically sync with FamilyWall
  • Subscribed calendars (like school calendars) are read-only
  • You can customize the color for each calendar to easily identify events
  • All family members can see shared calendars

How to Connect Calendars

1

Connect Google Calendar

Click "Connect Another Calendar" and select Google. Sign in with your Google account and authorize FamilyWall.

2

Subscribe to Public Calendars

Paste the calendar URL (ending in .ics) in the "Subscribe to a Calendar" section. Name your calendar and choose a color.

3

Import School Schedules

Many schools provide calendar URLs. Ask your school administrator for the calendar URL and add it as a subscribed calendar.
